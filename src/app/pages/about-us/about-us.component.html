The Journey

Founded in 2005, Autocare Towing started with a single flatbed truck and a mission: to provide the most reliable and courteous towing service in the region. We recognized that vehicle breakdowns are stressful, and our goal was to alleviate that stress with prompt, professional assistance.

Over the years, we have grown into a fleet of state-of-the-art towing vehicles, capable of handling everything from motorcycles to heavy-duty commercial trucks. Our team of certified operators is trained to handle every situation with safety and efficiency as top priorities.
